A secret basement that was found at the end of a very tight hallway, found under under [[Locations/The Green Dolphin Tavern\|The Green Dolphin Tavern]] after it collapsed. It was broken up into two rooms, one that had a bunch of cages in it, and one that was locked behind a magic lock. The lock was opened up using the [[Items and Recipes/Odd necklace\|Odd necklace]] and revealed a room filled with withered people in it. One tried to kill us, and [[PC's/Caroline\|Caroline]], trying to protect [[PC's/Edward Ghoulious\|Edward Ghoulious]], accidently kills the rest of the ash people in the room. 
In this room, there was a desk, and in the desk was an escape route and the [[Items and Recipes/Super Healing Potion Recipe\|Super Healing Potion Recipe]]. One of the ash people was a police officers son, which caused the death of [[PC's/Edward Ghoulious\|Edward Ghoulious]].
